About Grand Luxe

A legacy of luxury, a commitment to excellence, and a passion for creating unforgettable memories.

Our Story

Founded in 1999 by visionary hotelier Elliot Grandmont, Grand Luxe Hotel was born from a simple dream: to create a place where every guest feels like royalty. What started as a boutique 50-room property has grown into a 350-room landmark of luxury hospitality.

Over 25 years, we've welcomed presidents, celebrities, and business leaders while always maintaining the personal touch that sets us apart. Every detail — from our handcrafted furnishings to our locally sourced cuisine — reflects our unwavering commitment to excellence.

Today, Grand Luxe stands as a testament to what hospitality can be when passion meets purpose. We don't just offer a place to sleep; we create experiences that last a lifetime.
